Rice

There are many different variations of rice. Today I will teach you how to make steamed white rice. Fun fact: Rice can be further catergorized as short or long grain.

Ingredients

    s
  • White rice short or long
  • Measuring cup
  • Rice cooker or a pot with a lid
  • Water
  • If cooking rice with a pot a towel that could cover the top of the pot is helpful!

Step-by-step (Rice Cooker)

  1. Measure out your cup/s of rice.
  2. Rinse the rice under cool or cold water up to three times.
  3. Measure out cool/cold water to the same ratio as the amount of rice you measured.
  4. Hit the cook button and wait.
  5. Once it finishes cooking, let the rice set for fifteen more minutes before stiring the rice through. This helps prevent the rice that's on the bottom not stick and burn.

Step-by-step (Pot)

  1. Measure out your cup/s of rice.
  2. Rinse the rice under cool or cold water up to three times.
  3. Measure out cool/cold water to the same ratio as the amount of rice you measured.
  4. Place your pot onto the stove top with it's lid covering it.
  5. OPTIONAL: Place the towel over the top of the pot and cover it with the lid.

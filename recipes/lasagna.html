Lasagna

Lasagna, the staple food for an American family in the tv shows. We will start with what you should get from the store. Then we will get into our portions and which order we will be putting in our ingredients. Seculant, cheesy, and meat filled excellency.

Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef and Italian sausage.
  • Onion and garlic.
  • Can of crushed tomatoes, tomatoe sauce, and tomato paste.
  • Sugar to enhance the flavor.
  • The seasonings we will be using are fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • I would recommend store bought Lasagna noodles.
  • We will use parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • Egg to stop the cheese from oozing when we cut into it.

Step-by-step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving so it can settle in.

Layering the lasagna

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the steps and finish the top off with the remaining Parmesan.

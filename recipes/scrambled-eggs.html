Scrambled Eggs

How do you like your eggs? I grew up on boiled and scrambled eggs. Today I will be teaching you how my late mother made her scrambled eggs. Growing up in an Asian house hold we really didn't use much if any measuring cups!

Ingredients

  • Eggs!
  • Salt
  • MSG
  • Any vegetable oil

Step-by-step

  1. Crack your eggs.
  2. Add salt and MSG.
  3. Scramble your eggs.
  4. Heat the pan up and add your vegetable oil.
  5. Once the oil is heated up add your eggs.
  6. Scramble the eggs to your liking, I prefer my scrambled eggs more golden brown!
